People Casting Call Filming Consent

Mariachi Melam is a film in production at Texas A&M University, College Station, developed as part of Jais George's Master of Science graduate thesis in Visualization. A filmmaker and engineer from India, Jais has previously worked as an assistant director and story artist in Malayalam cinema. His current work explores the intersection of 3D visual effects, virtual production, and generative AI in filmmaking.
