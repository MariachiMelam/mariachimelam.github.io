Mariachi Melam is a film in production at Texas A&M University, College Station, developed as part of Jais George's Master of Science graduate thesis in Visualization.

A filmmaker and engineer from India, Jais has previously worked as an assistant director and story artist in Malayalam cinema. Currently his work spans 3D visual effects, virtual production and generative AI in filmmaking. Mariachi Melam is his feature directorial debut
